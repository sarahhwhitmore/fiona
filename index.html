The Heartwarming Story of Fiona the Hippo

Many know of Fiona, the Cincinnati Zoo’s famously sassy hippo born in 2017. Born much too early to feed or walk on her own, her survival was unlikely. It was up to the teams at the Zoo and Cincinnati Children’s Hospital to help her overcome the odds.
